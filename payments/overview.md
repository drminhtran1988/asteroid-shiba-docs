# Payments & Withdrawals

Asteroid Shiba runs on two currencies, kept cleanly separate: **USDT** for paying, **ASTRO** for playing.

## What you pay with — USDT (BEP20)

Scanner upgrades are bought **directly with USDT on BNB Smart Chain (BEP20)**. There's no balance to top up and manage — you pay for an upgrade, you get it. Simple.

## What you earn — ASTRO

Everything you earn inside the game is **ASTRO** — from discovering asteroids and from your portfolio's daily mining. ASTRO is the in-game currency: spend it on [Research](../research/overview.md) to grow your income, or withdraw it.

## Withdrawing your earnings

When you're ready to cash out, your **ASTRO is converted to USDT (BEP20)** at a set rate and sent to your BSC wallet — once you reach the minimum withdrawal amount.

{% hint style="info" %}
The exact conversion rate, minimum withdrawal, fees, and timing are being finalized and will be published here before launch. *(Reference scale: 1 ASTRO = $0.001.)*
{% endhint %}

## In short

* **Pay** in USDT (BEP20) — for Scanner upgrades.
* **Earn** ASTRO — inside the game.
* **Withdraw** — convert ASTRO → USDT (BEP20) to your wallet.

# Ambassador program

For **influencers, partners, and community leaders** who bring explorers to Asteroid Shiba. Ambassadors earn more on every referral — and help grow the hunt.

## What Ambassadors earn

Higher commission at every level (vs. the standard 8/4/2):

| Level | Ambassador commission |
| ----- | --------------------- |
| L1 (direct) | **10%** |
| L2 | **6%** |
| L3 | **3%** |

Up to **19%** of your network's Scanner-upgrade spend — paid to you **instantly in ASTRO**, on top, never taken from your referrals. Reinvest it in [Research](../research/overview.md) or withdraw it. See [How referrals work](overview.md).

## Who it's for

Creators, KOLs, project partners, and community leaders with a real audience — people who genuinely bring new explorers into the game.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Ambassador status is granted by hand — it can't be bought or unlocked in-game.** It's reserved for people who actually drive the community.
{% endhint %}

## How to become an Ambassador

Message our support and tell us about your audience and reach:

* 🛟 Support — [@shiba\_asteroid](https://t.me/shiba_asteroid)

If it's a fit, we'll grant Ambassador status to your account. Your network — and higher rates — carry into the game from day one.

# Founder Phase

![](../images/founder.png)

Before the game launches, Asteroid Shiba runs a **Founder Phase** — a limited window for early adopters to get in first.

## Founder Shiba — free, forever exclusive

Claim your **Founder Shiba**, an exclusive in-game mascot, for **free** during this phase.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Once the game launches, the Founder Shiba is **gone for good** — it will never be available again.
{% endhint %}

## Build your network before launch

The referral program is **already live**. Every explorer you invite now:

* carries into the game from **day one**,
* gives you a **stronger start**,
* and improves your **find-chance** through the [Explorer Network bonus](../referral/explorer-bonus.md).

Founders who build their network early start the season far ahead.

## Launch

**Asteroid Shiba launches July 24, 2026.** The Founder Phase runs until then.

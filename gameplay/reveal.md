# Discovery & Reveal

When a scan hits, the field goes dark, the rock cracks with light, and your asteroid is revealed — with its **rarity class**.

## The rarity classes

From most common to rarest:

* **Common** — the everyday find.
* **Rare** — a solid strike.
* **Epic** — genuinely valuable.
* **Legendary** — a jackpot moment.
* **Genesis** — the almost-mythical top tier. See [Genesis explained](../asteroids/genesis.md).

## Two rewards at once

The instant you discover an asteroid you get an **immediate ASTRO reward**. Then the asteroid joins your **[portfolio](portfolio.md)** and starts mining ASTRO for you every day it stays there.

See the exact numbers in **[What each is worth](../asteroids/values.md)**.

## Fair for everyone

The same asteroid pays the same to every player, regardless of Scanner level. Upgrades change your *odds* of finding rare asteroids — never the *reward* for finding one.

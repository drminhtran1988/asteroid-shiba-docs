# Home screen tour

Everything you need is on one screen. Here's what you're looking at.

* **Your Shiba & rocket** — your explorer, front and center. The rocket reflects your [Scanner](../scanner/overview.md) level.
* **Signals counter** — how many of your 5 daily [signals](../gameplay/signals-hunting.md) are left.
* **Hunt / Scan** — spend a signal and search the field.
* **Portfolio** — your collection of earning asteroids, and the ASTRO waiting to be collected. See [Your Portfolio](../gameplay/portfolio.md).
* **Invite Friends** — your referral link and network. See [How referrals work](../referral/overview.md).
* **Profile & wallet** — your avatar, stats, and BEP20 wallet.

{% hint style="info" %}
The scene grows with you: as your Scanner climbs, your Shiba's gear, rocket, and launch platform all upgrade — you're building a home base, not just a number.
{% endhint %}
